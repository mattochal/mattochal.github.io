About Me

I am a 2nd year PhD student (and 3rd year CDT student) at Edinburgh Centre for Robotics - a joint AI research centre between the University of Edinburgh and Heriot-Watt University, UK. I specialize in Few-Shot Learning, Meta-Learning, and Computer Vision algorithms and their real-world application for autonomous robots and systems.

My research is supervised by Dr Sen Wang and Prof Yvan Petillot from Heriot-Watt University, and jointly funded by SeeByte Ltd and EPSRC. I also work closely with Prof Amos Storkey and the BayesWatch research group at the University of Edinburgh.

During my undergrad, I worked with Prof Seb Stein, Dr Enrico Gerding from the University of Southampton, merging mechanism design and Reinforcement Learning for fair online resource allocation in computational clouds and clusters.

I also worked with Prof Markus Brede from the University of Southampton, studing game-theoretical propertise of a Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning algorithm - LOLA.
